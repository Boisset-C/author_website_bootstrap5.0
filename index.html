Geriatric Notes

About The Book

Geriatric Notes is an essential quick reference guide for those who provide care to the elderly both inpatient and outpatient. Designed to offer the basics with a practical, accessible, and concise approach that's easy to understand.

Who Wrote It

Geriatric Notes was written by Jamie W. Smith as a pocket size powerhouse resource guide for the clinician, student, or caregiver on the go. Whether on rounds, in class, or with a loved one, Geriatric Notes offers an easy to understand overview of most common diagnoses, topics, and symptoms encountered in the aging population.

Why Buy It

The 414 page book fits easily in a lab coat pocket, or a backpack. Sections include health promotion, neurology and psychiatry, cardiovascular, pulmonary, gastroenterology, genitourinary, rheumatology, dermatology, endocrinology, labs, Beers list, and common presentations. 4 different technical aspects???

About the Author

Jamie W. Smith grew up in Ferrum, VA and attended Franklin County High. She would countinue her education at Liberty University and graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing. After Liberty, Jamie would attend James Madison University and graduate with her Masters as a Family Nurse Practitioner. As a loving wife, and mother of two, Jamie seeks to add equity to her prefession and surrounding community. Starting out as a NP, Jamie struggled to find resources for clinical practice that were not overwhelming. As she would train new healthcare providers, they would ask for copies of her work. This would lead Jamie to take the next step and consider publishing her efforts. With the help of physician Dr. Bradley J. Goad, Jamie would go on to author Geriatric Notes.
