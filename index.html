Geriatric Notes

About The Book

Geriatric Notes is an essential quick reference guide for those who provide care to the elderly both inpatient and outpatient. Designed to offer the basics with a practical, accessible, and concise approach that's easy to understand.

Who Wrote It

Geriatric Notes was written by Jamie W. Smith as a pocket size powerhouse resource guide for the clinician, student, or caregiver on the go. Whether on rounds, in class, or with a loved one, Geriatric Notes offers an easy to understand overview of most common diagnoses, topics, and symptoms encountered in the aging population.

Why Buy It

The 414 page book fits easily in a lab coat pocket, or a backpack.
